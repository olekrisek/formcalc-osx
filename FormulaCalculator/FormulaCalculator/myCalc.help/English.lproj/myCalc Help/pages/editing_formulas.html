Editing formulas

How easy isn't it to do mistakes. As you can see the formula as you type in the bottom text field, it is easy to correct mistakes. Press the “EDIT” button above the text field, and edit the text as you like. When you save the text, the calculator will re-evaluate the equation and display the new result.
