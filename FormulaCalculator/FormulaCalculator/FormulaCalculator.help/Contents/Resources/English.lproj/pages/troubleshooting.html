Troubleshooting

Q: The key-bindings does not work anymore - How do I get it back. ? 

A: When you enter numbers in the variable fields, the key bindings for the buttons stop working. It have to be so, since the key-strokes on your keyboard should move into your variable field, and not activate the buttons. Pressing one button on the calculator resets this behaviour, so you can continue with the eyboard if you like.
