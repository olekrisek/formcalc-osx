Financial functions



You find the financial functions on the "finance" button. A drawer on the right side opens.

In this version, there is three groups of functions,

  1. Annuity loan calculations

  2. Future value and interest calculations

  3. Future value of savings calculations.

Each group contains a number of formulas to use.
