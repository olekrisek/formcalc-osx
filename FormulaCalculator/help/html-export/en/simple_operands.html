Simple operands

Simple operands. 
Those are the basic + - * / and ^. The last one is "Power of". 

The calculation engine takes one operand at a time, and complete the calculation. The priority of evaluation is: 
  1. Parenthesis
  2. Power of
  3. Multiply and divide
  4. Plus and minus. 
This means, that content in parenthesis are always calculated first. Then Power of operators, then multiply and divide, and finally plus and minus operators.
