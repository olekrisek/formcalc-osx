Priorities of operands

The calculation engine respect all the regular priorities of operands, writing 2+3*4 gives the result of 14, since 3*4 is calculated first, and then adding 2. opposite 4*3+2 also gives 14,  

If you want 3 to be added to 2, and then multiplied with 4, you have to use parenthesis, like (2+3)*4. 

You can of course split the equation into two, pressing “=” sign after 2+3, and then *4. This is pretty standard.
